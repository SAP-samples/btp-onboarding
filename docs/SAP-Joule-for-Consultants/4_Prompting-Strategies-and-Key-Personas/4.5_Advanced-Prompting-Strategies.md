### Advanced Prompting Strategies for Joule Consultants

The table specifies the advanced strategies to keep in mind while creating prompts, how this is reflected in a prompt, and why this strategy is important to follow while making a prompt.

| Strategy | Examples | Why? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Explicitly Request Multi-Document Summarization | <ul><li>Summarize insights from multiple sources on how SAP ISBN improves supplier collaboration across industries.</li><li>Summarize insights from multiple sources on how SAP PSM improves public sector budgeting efficiency.</li></ul> | Ensures the system retrieves and synthesizes multiple chunks rather than relying on just one. |
| Use Comparisons in Prompts | <ul><li>Compare SAP BRIM and SAP RAR in handling recurring revenue streams.</li><li>Compare SAP BTP's Kyma runtime with traditional SAP cloud deployment options.</li></ul> | Forces retrieval of documents across different perspectives. |
| Ask for Supporting Evidence | <ul><li>Provide documented examples of companies using SAP BTP for enterprise automation.</li><li>Provide real-world examples of how SAP Predictive Maintenance reduced downtime in manufacturing plants.</li></ul> | Encourages AI to prioritize retrieval of factual, document-backed responses rather than just generating a guess. |
| Enforce Step-by-Step Reasoning | <ul><li>Explain the step-by-step process for setting up SAP BRIM for subscription-based businesses.</li><li>Explain step-by-step how SAP ISBN integrates with SAP Business Network for supplier collaboration.</li></ul> | Encourages logical structuring of retrieved information and works well for process-driven queries (Q2C, Finance workflows, BTP integrations, etc.). |
| Generate Actionable Recommendations | <ul><li>Suggest three best practices for implementing SAP BTP for keeping a clean core S/4HANA system.</li><li>Suggest three ways SAP Finance can optimize cost allocation in large enterprises.</li></ul> | Retrieves prescriptive, solution-driven insights and helps consultants make informed decisions. |
