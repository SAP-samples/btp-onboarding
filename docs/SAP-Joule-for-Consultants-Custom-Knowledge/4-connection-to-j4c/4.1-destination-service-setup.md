## Configure the Destination Service

The connection between AI Core Document Grounding and SAP Joule for Consultants is established via a Destination Service. The steps below describe how to configure this Destination Service.

- In the subaccount, go to **Instances and Subscriptions** and click **Create**.
- Select the service **Destination Service** with the plan `lite`, fill in the required fields and click **Create** to create the instance.

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/destination-service-creation.png" width="80%">
</p>

- Click on the Destination Service instance (not the blue text).
- In the section **Service Keys**, click **Create**.
- Pick a name and click **Create**.

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/destination-service-service-key.png" width="80%">
</p>

- Download the Service Key, as it will be needed for the connection to SAP Joule for Consultants.
- Click on the blue text of the instance to proceed with the configuration of the destinations.

> **Note:** Destinations can be configured either at the instance level or at the subaccount level. In this setup, the destinations will be configured at the instance level.

> **Note:** For editing and deleting destinations, you will need the BTP role collection `Destination Administrator`.

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/destination-service-download.png" width="80%">
</p>

## Create the Destination

- In the Destination Service instance, choose the section **Destinations** and click **Create**.
- Choose **From Scratch** and click **Create**.

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/destination-service-create-scratch.png" width="80%">
</p>

- Get the Service Key of the AI Core instance.
- Choose **OAuth2ClientCredentials** as authentication and fill in the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
- Add these values to the fields using the information from the AI Core Service Key:
  - **Token Service URL:** `<url>/oauth/token`
  - **URL:** `<AI_API_URL>/v2/lm/document-grounding`
- Add the following **Additional Properties**:
  - `HTML5.ForwardAuthToken`: `true`
  - `HTML5.DynamicDestination`: `true`
  - `AI-Resource-Group`: name of the resource group

> **Note:** The property `AI-Resource-Group` is not available in the value help.

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/destination-service-create-destination.png" width="80%">
</p>

- Check the connection for the destination.

</p>
