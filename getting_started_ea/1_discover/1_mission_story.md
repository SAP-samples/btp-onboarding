## Welcome and Target Group for this Mission


Welcome to the SAP Discovery Center mission **"Getting Started with SAP BTP Enterprise Accounts"**!

### About this Mission

This mission is designed for **global account administrators** and **IT contacts** who received a new SAP BTP **Enterprise Account**. 

In case you do not have an Enterprise Account and if you wish to explore/buy an Enterprise Account, you may refer to a [pay-as-you-go license](https://www.sap.com/products/technology-platform/trial.html) and use [Free Tier Service Plans](https://discovery-center.cloud.sap/viewServices?provider=all&regions=all&category=freetierservices). See also the card "Prerequisites: Learn about the Free Tier model".

### Target Audience

We aim towards all personas looking to get started with SAP BTP to help drive your company's digital transformation. This mission will provide you with the basic building blocks to equip you with a smooth start to your platform journey. Topics covered in this mission include:

- Introduction to the SAP Business Technology Platform
- Commercial models to run an Enterprise Account
- Global Accounts and (regional) Subaccounts
- Provider and Regions and 
- Runtime Environments
- Navigating SAP support


### Alternative for Single User Accounts

For developers and single users, who want a jump start, we recommend starting on the [SAP Business Technology Platform Trial](https://cockpit.hanatrial.ondemand.com/trial/#/home/trial) page. Try out the tutorial [Get a Free Account on SAP BTP Trial](https://developers.sap.com/tutorials/hcp-create-trial-account.html) in the [SAP Developer Center](https://developers.sap.com/).
